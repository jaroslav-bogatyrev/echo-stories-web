## Improving Rankings

### 1. Content Strategy
**Add more pages:**
- News/blog about projects and festivals
- Team member bios
- Project development updates
- Behind-the-scenes content

**Update frequency:** At least monthly

**Why it works:** Fresh content = more frequent crawls = better rankings

### 2. Build Backlinks
Get other websites to link to yours:

**Film Directories:**
- IMDb Pro (company page)
- Cineuropa
- Cinando
- Czech Film Center
- European Film Academy

**Social Media:**
- LinkedIn company page
- Share projects on Facebook, Instagram
- Film industry groups

**Press & Media:**
- Festival selections → press releases
- Submit to film blogs and magazines
- Industry news sites

**Why it works:** Backlinks = #1 ranking factor

### 3. Optimize Project Pages
Add more content to each film page:
- Director's statement (200+ words)
- Production notes
- Cast & crew details
- Festival selections
- Press quotes
- Behind-the-scenes photos

**Target:** 500-800 words per project page

### 4. Local SEO (Prague)
**Google Business Profile:**
- Create profile: https://business.google.com/
- Add Prague location
- Upload photos
- Link to website

**Result:** Appear in Google Maps + local searches

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Pages Not Indexing
1. Check Search Console → Coverage
2. Verify robots.txt allows crawling
3. Re-request indexing
4. Wait 1-2 weeks

### No Organic Traffic
1. Build more backlinks
2. Add more content
3. Optimize existing pages
4. Check keywords in Search Console

### Falling Rankings
1. Add fresh content
2. Update old pages
3. Build new backlinks
4. Check for technical errors
